How It Works

Our blockchain-based system ensures end-to-end traceability of products across the entire supply chain

Step 1: Manufacturer Registration

Manufacturers register new products on the blockchain with unique identifiers, creating a permanent and verifiable record of origin.

Step 2: Seller Verification

Authorized sellers confirm receipt and transfer ownership of products through secure blockchain transactions, ensuring chain of custody.

Step 3: Consumer Authentication

Consumers verify product authenticity by scanning QR codes or entering product serial numbers, instantly revealing the full product history.
